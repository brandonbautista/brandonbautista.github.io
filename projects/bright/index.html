BRIGHT: Stories of Hope & Innovation in Michigan Classrooms

Award-winning podcast celebrating Michigan educators — built for goodwill, not pitches.

At a Glance

What

BRIGHT is a podcast and blog series highlighting innovative Michigan teachers. Each episode shares classroom-tested ideas and human stories other educators can adapt quickly — all while lifting the public perception of Michigan Virtual as a collaborative partner to schools.

The show generated positive press coverage statewide, engaging communities connected to featured educators.

Why

We set out to serve — not sell — by centering educator voices and reframing Michigan Virtual as a partner to schools, not a competitor.

  • Built goodwill by spotlighting teachers doing remarkable work.
  • Earned local and statewide press coverage through outreach and press releases.
  • Created a lasting positive shift in how Michigan Virtual is perceived.

Impact

217,000+ listens/views across YouTube and major podcast platforms.

  • Award: Best Educational Podcast — 37th Annual Educational Advertising Awards.
  • Positive shift in public perception and strengthened relationships with schools.
