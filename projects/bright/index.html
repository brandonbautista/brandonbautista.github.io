At a Glance

What

BRIGHT is a podcast and blog series highlighting innovative Michigan teachers. Each episode shares classroom-tested ideas and human stories other educators can adapt quickly — and elevates Michigan Virtual as a collaborative partner to schools.

We also activated regional pride by pitching local press for each featured educator, generating positive coverage across the state.

Why

We set out to serve — not sell. By centering educator voices, we reframed Michigan Virtual from “competitor” to partner and built durable goodwill in the K-12 community.

  • Spotlighted teachers doing remarkable work.
  • Proactive outreach to newspapers, TV, and community press.
  • Aligned with inbound strategy: useful content first; trust follows.

Impact

217,000+ listens/views across YouTube and major podcast platforms.

  • Award: Best Educational Podcast — 37th Annual Educational Advertising Awards.
  • Lifted public perception; strengthened partnerships statewide.
