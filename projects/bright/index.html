BRIGHT: Stories of Hope & Innovation in Michigan Classrooms

Award-winning podcast celebrating Michigan educators — built for goodwill, not pitches.

At a Glance

217,000+ listens/views Best Educational Podcast — 37th Annual Educational Advertising Awards Press coverage across Michigan

What

BRIGHT is a podcast and blog series that shines a light on outstanding Michigan educators. Each episode pairs a candid interview with practical, classroom-tested ideas so peers can try something new the next day.

Beyond episodes, we packaged every story with social clips, quotes, and visual assets to extend reach and keep the focus on the teachers themselves — not on selling anything.

Why

Michigan Virtual succeeds only when local districts succeed. Historically, some perceived us as a competitor; we set out to change that by serving — not selling — and by elevating educator voices in their own communities.

  • Reposition Michigan Virtual as a partner, not a rival, to Michigan schools.
  • Build goodwill through consistent, useful content that creates real audience affinity.
  • Run localized outreach (press releases, pitches to newspapers & TV) for each featured educator to generate positive coverage at the community level.

On a personal note, I’m proud we used our platform to celebrate teachers — a profession too often in the news for the wrong reasons.

Impact

217,000+ listens/views across YouTube and major podcast platforms, with strong completion and subscriber growth signaling sustained value.

  • Award: Best Educational Podcast — 37th Annual Educational Advertising Awards.
  • Perception shift: Clear movement toward viewing Michigan Virtual as a collaborative partner to districts.
  • Visibility: Recurring local press mentions tied to featured educators amplified community goodwill.
