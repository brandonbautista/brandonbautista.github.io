BRIGHT: Stories of Hope & Innovation in Michigan Classrooms

Award-winning podcast celebrating Michigan educators — built for goodwill, not pitches.

At a Glance

What

BRIGHT is a podcast and blog series featuring Michigan teachers known for innovative classroom practices. Each episode shares practical ideas and first-hand stories other educators can adapt quickly.

Format: interview-driven storytelling with classroom examples and takeaways.

Why

We wanted to provide genuine value to educators and build trust with the community by spotlighting great work — whether or not it involved Michigan Virtual. Guided by inbound marketing training, we focused on service, not selling.

  • Champion educator voice and customer advocacy over brand promotion.
  • Create affinity and goodwill through consistent, useful content.
  • Model pedagogical best practices and technology in action.

Impact

217,000+ listens/views across YouTube and major podcast platforms, indicating strong resonance with our audience of Michigan educators interested in technology and pedagogy.

  • Recognition: Best Educational Podcast — 37th Annual Educational Advertising Awards.
  • Community impact: Elevated teacher stories and strengthened goodwill toward Michigan Virtual.

About the Show

Michigan is home to so many outstanding educators. In our podcast and blog series — BRIGHT: Stories of Hope & Innovation in Michigan Classrooms — we talk to inspirational Michigan educators known for their innovative approach to learning and hear first-hand what their classrooms look like, how these changes impact students, and what advice they have for fellow teachers looking to try something new.
