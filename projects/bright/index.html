BRIGHT: Stories of Hope & Innovation in Michigan Classrooms

Award-winning podcast celebrating Michigan educators — built for goodwill, not pitches.

At a Glance

217,000+ listens/views Best Educational Podcast — Ed. Advertising Awards Press coverage statewide

What

BRIGHT is a podcast and blog series highlighting innovative Michigan teachers. Every episode brings forward classroom-tested ideas and human stories other educators can apply quickly.

Format: interview-led storytelling with practical takeaways.

Why

We set out to serve — not sell — by centering educator voices and modeling our role as a partner to schools. Historically, some viewed Michigan Virtual as a competitor; this series helped reset that perception.

  • Built goodwill by spotlighting teachers doing remarkable work (often outside our programs).
  • Reframed Michigan Virtual as a collaborator invested in educator success statewide.
  • Created a steady drumbeat of helpful content aligned to inbound best practices.

Impact

217,000+ listens/views across YouTube and podcast platforms; strong subscriber growth and completion rates indicated sustained value.

  • Awards: Best Educational Podcast (37th Educational Advertising Awards).
  • Public perception: Clear shift toward seeing Michigan Virtual as a partner to local districts.
  • Press & community: Targeted outreach to local media for each featured teacher generated steady coverage and community engagement.

I’m most proud that we used our platform to celebrate educators — bringing more positive attention to a profession too often in the headlines for the wrong reasons.
