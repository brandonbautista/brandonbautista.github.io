What

A thought-leadership essay on how guilt-driven fundraising can erode trust — paired with practical steps for relationship-based donor communications.

Why

Impact

My Approach

The craft behind the content

🎯

Turn complexity into clarity

Complex emotions become plain-spoken, useful content. No jargon. No condescension. Just clarity.

⚖️

Provoke without alienating

I balance provocation with credibility to spark conversation, using light humor to disarm defensiveness and invite reflection.

🤝

Earn trust before driving action

The most persuasive writing doesn't feel persuasive. It feels like a friend giving good advice.

"What I need is a relationship, not a drip campaign… The most meaningful giving doesn't come from being made to feel bad. It comes from feeling seen."
