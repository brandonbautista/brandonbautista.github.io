Overview

Utility over theory

Our goal was utility over theory: make it fast and easy for anyone — designers, writers, or partners — to produce work that is unmistakably Michigan Virtual.

Problem
Without a centralized system, teams and vendors were producing inconsistent work. Every project had to re-establish the basics — wasting time and creating brand drift.
Solution
We centralized everything on a clean, public-facing page and paired it with ready-to-use files. Standards only work when they're easy to find and easier to follow.

Impact

Standards that actually get used

Standards only work when they're easy to find and easier to follow. We centralized everything on a clean, public-facing page and paired it with ready-to-use files. As a result, the brand has been consistently applied across teams and vendors — with fewer revision cycles, fewer one-off questions, and more beautiful, accessible work.
