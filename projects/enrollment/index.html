Overview

Out-connect, not outspend

What
Always-on enrollment marketing across the school year — launch awareness, value spotlights (e.g., unique courses), and urgency messaging as deadlines approach. Channels: Email to school contacts · Facebook campaigns to MI educators & families · Short videos to inform & disarm skepticism.
Why
We won't outspend national competitors, but we can out-connect: speak educators' language, position as partner (not vendor), and be remarkably useful. Goals: Align to school calendars and workload realities · Lift perception of Michigan Virtual as a collaborative ally · Meet or exceed enrollment targets consistently.
Impact
High-efficiency Facebook performance at modest budgets, strong email engagement, and repeatable semester wins. Key results: CTR ~0.24% · CPC ~$0.30 · Reach ~296K · 11.7K link clicks.
