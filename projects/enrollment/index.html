Winter Semester 2025 Enrollment Campaign

Awareness → value props → urgency. Multi-channel push across paid social, email, and video to hit enrollment goals during the first weeks of January.

At a Glance

Context

Michigan Virtual’s second semester begins late January. Districts return from winter break right after New Year’s, so we launch enrollment immediately to meet ambitious goals despite bigger-budget competitors.

  • Audience: Michigan educators & enrollment coordinators
  • Timing: First 2–3 weeks of January
  • Phases: Open now → course highlights → last-chance urgency

Approach

Multi-channel cadence to meet educators where they are.

  • Email blasts & reminders to school contacts
  • Facebook ads targeted to MI educators
  • Video that entertains (course selection) and explains (how MV works)

Impact

We consistently hit enrollment goals through service-forward messaging and partner positioning—never just “vendor.”

  • Efficient paid social: low CPC for statewide reach
  • Emails with ~2× typical opens for this space
  • Reinforced at conferences & community touchpoints

Paid Social Performance (Facebook)

Campaign spanned awareness, course highlights, and urgency creatives.

Reach: 296,511 Impressions: 5,022,928 Link Clicks: 11,700 (9,652 unique) CPC: $0.30 Spend: $3,499.99

What it shows

With a modest budget, we reached the statewide educator community and drove five million+ impressions at a very efficient $0.30 CPC. This kept the top of the funnel full while emails and urgency ads closed the loop.

How We Sequenced the Campaign

Week 1: Open Now

Announce enrollment and nudge returning districts to act quickly after break.

  • Facebook awareness + email announcement
  • Partner outreach & conference presence reinforce message

Week 2: Value Props

Highlight unique, relevant courses and student outcomes; drive clicks to catalog.

  • Course-feature ads & social carousels
  • Explainer video for admins & counselors

Week 3: Urgency

“Last chance” creatives and reminder emails to ensure all enrollments are captured.

  • Retargeting + countdown messaging
  • Final reminder to curated email segments
