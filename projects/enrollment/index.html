Yearlong Enrollment Cycle

Spring • Summer • Fall • Winter — a sustained, multi-channel campaign that meets educators when they return from break, showcases unique courses, and drives on-time enrollments.

At a Glance

Multi-channel: Facebook, Email, Video Partner-first positioning Awareness → Consideration → Action

What

A yearlong enrollment engine aligned to school rhythms. Each term we sequenced three moments: (1) enrollment now open, (2) spotlights on surprising/valuable courses, and (3) closing soon urgency.

Why

We can’t outspend large competitors — so we out-connect. We speak educators’ language, show real value, and reinforce that Michigan Virtual is a partner, not a vendor.

Impact

Consistently met ambitious enrollment goals through coherent, audience-first storytelling and channel orchestration across Facebook, targeted email, and video.
