Michigan Cares: Statewide SEL Awareness & Adoption

Multi-channel campaign to scale evidence-based SEL access, build goodwill, and drive usage across Michigan schools.

At a Glance

1.04M FB impressions, 6,346 clicks 19.1M RTB impressions 2.145M broadcast impressions 4,086 users/lessons downloaded

What

A statewide campaign to promote Michigan Cares — an SEL portal for students, families, and educators — using paid digital, broadcast, PR, and lifecycle email. Work spanned strategy and execution: creative, landing content, social video scripts, postcard + flyer, surveys, analytics, and a toolkit for partners.

  • 9 social video scripts, email drip content, parent flyer, partner postcard.
  • PR consultation and outreach support; user data analysis & reporting.
  • Always-on optimization across Facebook + RTB; broadcast via MAB.

Why

Our intent was service over sell: expand access to SEL at scale and position Michigan Virtual as a partner to districts, not a competitor. We centered district needs and made it simple to adopt — including grant-driven access to premium features — while building goodwill with positive, educator-first storytelling.

  • Reframed brand perception as collaborator to local schools.
  • Grant messaging to unlock premium, data-driven SEL for districts.
  • Direct outreach + press mentions to raise local community visibility.

Impact

Strong paid reach and engagement; statewide broadcast coverage; thousands of lesson downloads; clear signals of growing awareness and adoption.

  • Facebook: 1,039,818 impressions • 6,346 clicks (0.61% CTR).
  • RTB: 19,100,654 impressions • 3,079 clicks (0.08% CTR).
  • Broadcast: 2,145,000 impressions; $30k buy returned ~$97,374 value.
  • Usage: 4,086 users/lessons downloaded to date.
