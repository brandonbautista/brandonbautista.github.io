Michigan Cares: Statewide SEL & Mental Health Awareness

Funded $100,000 to help districts provide free SEL resources during COVID lockdowns — we delivered a multi-audience campaign to drive statewide adoption.

At a Glance

$100,000 program funding Statewide, district-wide access COVID lockdown support

What

Michigan Cares offered free, evidence-based SEL & mental health content for districts, parents, and students across the state. Our campaign mobilized email, webinars, and inbound content to drive awareness and sign-ups.

Why

During lockdowns, districts needed fast, credible SEL support. We sought to serve communities first — not sell — and reinforce Michigan Virtual as a partner to schools, not a competitor.

  • Center families and educators with practical, free resources.
  • Align to district needs by segment (admins, educators, parents).
  • Build goodwill and reframe public perception through consistent, positive outreach.

Impact

  • Statewide reach: multi-audience email series; on-demand webinars; clear admin activation path.
  • High engagement: 49,658 sends with 35.92% opens and 2.89% clicks across representative sets.
  • Goodwill lift: reinforced Michigan Virtual as a collaborator helping schools support SEL during a critical period.
