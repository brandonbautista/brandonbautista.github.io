Michigan Cares: Statewide SEL Awareness & Adoption

Multi-channel campaign during COVID to support schools, families, and students with free, evidence-based SEL & mental-health resources.

At a Glance

$100,000 state funding Free access for all MI districts COVID-era community support Press outreach & media coverage

What

Michigan Cares offered Michigan schools, families, and students a free portal of evidence-based SEL and mental-health resources. Our remit: make every district aware and help them launch quickly.

More

We built a campaign that combined awareness, enablement, and clear next steps for admins, educators, and parents.

Why

During lockdown, districts needed ways to support social-emotional wellbeing. Our objective: drive awareness and adoption statewide—equipping local leaders with ready-to-use content and a simple path to launch.

More
  • Serve, don’t sell: lead with help and clarity.
  • Tailor by audience (admins, educators, parents).
  • Blend paid, owned, and earned media for reach + credibility.

Impact

State-level awareness, strong email engagement, and meaningful uptake of free portals.

More
  • Press mentions from targeted local outreach to newspapers, TV, and radio.
  • District toolkits + drip communications accelerated adoption and usage.
  • Positive feedback from school leaders navigating a difficult moment.
