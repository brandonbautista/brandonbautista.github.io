Overview

Awareness, enablement, and action

What & Why

Michigan Cares offered Michigan schools, families, and students a free portal of evidence-based SEL and mental-health resources. Our remit: make every district aware and help them launch quickly. During lockdown, districts needed support for social-emotional wellbeing. We led with service, not sales.

$100,000 state funding
Free access for all MI districts
COVID-era community support
Press outreach & media coverage

My Approach

Serve, don't sell: lead with help and clarity. Tailor by audience (admins, educators, parents). Blend paid, owned, and earned media for reach + credibility.

How We Reached Them

Six channels, one message

✉️

Email (Segmented)

Audience-specific messages to admins, educators, and parents with clear paths to request/access their portal. (Blasts + Drips + Webinar Invites + Reminders)

📣

Facebook Ads

Paid social to drive awareness and interest among educators and families with supportive, service-forward copy. (Awareness + Remarketing)

📻

TV & Radio PSAs

Broadcast placements to reach households statewide — inviting families and schools to explore the free resource. (Statewide Reach + Public Service Announcements)

📰

Press & Outreach

Targeted media outreach and press releases to local markets featuring the schools/educators highlighted in communications. (Local News + Community Coverage)

🧰

District Enablement

Step-by-step toolkits and FAQs to launch quickly and communicate confidently with their communities. (Toolkit + FAQs + Templates)

Inbound Content
