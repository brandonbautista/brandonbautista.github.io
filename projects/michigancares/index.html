Michigan Cares (Statewide SEL & Mental Health)

A state-funded, multi-channel campaign during COVID lockdowns to help districts launch free SEL portals for students and families.

At a Glance

State funding: $100,000 49,658+ emails delivered Facebook: 1,039,818 impressions RTB Display: 19,100,654 impressions TV & Radio: $30k media — $97k value

What

Michigan Cares provided every Michigan district with free, customizable SEL and mental health content for students and parents during COVID lockdowns. We built a statewide awareness campaign to help leaders launch portals quickly and support their communities when it mattered most.

Why

Our mission was to reach educators and families where they already were and make adoption effortless. We blended service-first messaging with clear, practical steps to help districts stand up portals fast.

  • Emphasize access for every district—no cost barriers.
  • Reach admins, educators, and parents with tailored messages.
  • Use trusted channels (email, local media, and social) to build momentum.

Impact

Meaningful reach and engagement across channels, strong email performance, and wide paid-media coverage helped districts adopt SEL resources quickly statewide.

  • 49,658+ emails delivered to targeted segments.
  • 1.04M Facebook impressions; 19.1M display impressions.
  • $30k TV/Radio spend generated $97k+ in value statewide.
