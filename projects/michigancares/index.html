Michigan Cares: Statewide SEL & Mental Health Awareness

Funded $100,000 to help every district provide free SEL resources during COVID lockdowns — delivered through a robust, multi-platform outreach campaign.

At a Glance

$100,000 funding Statewide, district-wide access Multi-platform campaign

What

Michigan Cares provided free, evidence-based SEL and mental health content to all Michigan districts, parents, and students. The campaign deployed a coordinated mix of:

  • Segmented email campaigns for administrators, educators, and parents
  • Press releases and targeted local media outreach
  • Inbound blog content for awareness
  • On-demand and live webinars for engagement

Why

During the lockdown phase of COVID, SEL needs surged. This initiative supported districts in caring for their communities — reinforcing Michigan Virtual’s role as a partner to public schools, not a competitor.

  • Provide meaningful, free resources in a time of need
  • Lift public perception through visible, positive action
  • Reach local communities via press mentions in educators’ hometowns

Impact

  • Statewide reach with consistent messaging across multiple channels
  • Increased goodwill and strengthened relationships with districts
  • Highlighted educators and their work in a positive, human way
  • Robust engagement from email to webinars to press coverage
