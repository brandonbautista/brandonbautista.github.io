How I Work

Culture is a performance strategy

The best marketing teams I've seen — and the one I was part of building — aren't defined by headcount or budget. They're defined by trust, clear communication, and a shared sense of purpose. I was fortunate to learn how to lead from someone who modeled it well. When she moved on, I tried to carry that forward — adding to the team deliberately, building an environment where people from across the organization came when they were stuck. When you're lean and competing against organizations with more resources, that kind of culture isn't a nice-to-have. It's a performance strategy.
